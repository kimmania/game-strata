Strata

Ice-Core Sampling

You are a research scientist at Arctic Station Aurora, extracting drill cores from the polar ice shelf. Each transparent tube preserves a vertical timeline of geological epochs — bands of ice, volcanic ash, clay, and ancient pollen.

Your job is to clean and catalog the cores: separate each sediment color into its own tube. Use Tamp to compress completed bottom blocks and recover precious vertical space.

How to Play

Sampling

Tap a source core tube to select it. Then tap a destination tube to pour the top sediment layers.

You may only pour onto a matching color or into an empty tube.

Block Pouring

When you pour, all matching layers at the top move together as one block.

Tamping

Select a tube with a bottom block of matching layers (2+), then press Tamp to compress them into one dense layer. This frees up vertical space.

Tamp charges are limited per level!

Goal

Every non-empty tube must contain only one uniform sediment color. Empty tubes are fine.

Complete within target moves with leftover tamp charges for 3 stars.
